Key inherited rules:

- Source links are original reference links for attribution and traceability.
- Links alone are not complete source material.
- Do not infer facts from URLs, page titles, or link slugs.
- Source content may include article text, news summaries, extracted webpage content, manual notes, screenshot OCR, or structured facts.
- Empty, unavailable, unreadable, link-only, or unrelated source material must produce `<ERROR>Insufficient source material</ERROR>`.
- Partially relevant material can be used only for facts it clearly supports.
